Request iPhone access

Voxhora's iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch apps ship through Apple's TestFlight program. Tell us who you are and we'll send you a TestFlight invite at the email address you provide. Approvals are manual — typically same-day.

When you click Send, your email app will open with a pre-filled message addressed to Patrick. Hit Send and you're done — approval usually within a day.

What happens next

  1. Patrick reviews your request (usually same-day).
  2. Apple sends a TestFlight invite to the email address you provided.
  3. You install Apple's free TestFlight app from the App Store on your iPhone.
  4. You tap the invite link from the email; TestFlight installs Voxhora for you.
  5. Open Voxhora and sign in with your Apple ID. Your iPad and Apple Watch pick it up automatically — same Apple ID, same Voxhora.

Why TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's official app-distribution program. It's free, secure, and bundled with iCloud — you install Apple's TestFlight app once, accept your invite, and Voxhora installs and auto-updates from there, with none of the friction of the App Store review cycle.
