Updates

Voxhora is built by one practicing attorney, in working sessions between client obligations. Here's how releases reach you and what's shipped recently.

Release cadence

Mac releases land via the auto-updater whenever they're ready — sometimes weekly during heavy feature work, sometimes monthly during stability passes. Your Mac picks them up on its next launch (or you can force a check via Voxhora menu → Check for Updates…).

iPhone, iPad, and Watch releases go through Apple's TestFlight system. Apple does a one-time "Beta App Review" the first time you receive a build (typically 24–48 hours); subsequent builds reach you within minutes of upload. Each TestFlight build expires after 90 days, so you'll get a refresh notification at least once a quarter.

Get in touch

Feedback, bug reports, feature requests, or just want to talk shop: support@voxhora.app.

If you've found a bug, a quick description of what you did and what happened is usually enough. Screenshots help. Voxhora doesn't phone home with crash reports automatically — your privacy beats remote diagnostics.
