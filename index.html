Your data is safe

Voxhora is built for the ethical obligations attorneys actually carry. Texas Disciplinary Rule 1.05 takes client confidentiality seriously; so do we. Four guarantees, in plain English:

Encrypted in transit and at rest

Every byte of your client data is encrypted on the wire (TLS 1.3) between your device and Apple's iCloud, and encrypted at rest inside Apple's storage. Apple's Advanced Data Protection for iCloud (Settings → iCloud → Advanced Data Protection) raises that to full end-to-end encryption if you turn it on — recommended for any attorney handling sensitive matters.

Your data is never lost

Your data lives on your Mac, your iPhone, your iPad, and your Apple Watch — a full local copy on each device — and is replicated to Apple's iCloud as the master. Drop your iPhone in the lake; your Mac still has every billable entry. Lose every device you own — buy a brand-new Mac or iPhone, install Voxhora, sign into the same Apple ID, and everything repopulates to its last state automatically: every client, note, document, to-do, and billable entry, back within minutes. There is nothing to manually back up and nothing to restore by hand.

Always retrievable, in formats you control

Voxhora's voucher CSV export lets you pull your billable record out at any time to any folder, drive, or cloud you choose. Per-client documents (PCs, intake letters, anything you've uploaded) live in your own iCloud Drive — visible in Finder, the Files app, drag-and-droppable anywhere. Your data is never trapped in a Voxhora-only format.

No Voxhora server. No Voxhora staff with access.

There is no Voxhora central database for a hacker to breach because there is no central database. No "support engineer" at Voxhora can read your client list. No third-party analytics, no advertising trackers, no data sharing. Apple is your storage provider; Voxhora is just the software you run on your own devices.

Tamper-evident audit chain

Every billable line item, every agent-driven intake, every voucher export is hash-chained to the prior one. The chain itself is tamper-evident — if a row gets altered after the fact, the chain breaks visibly. FDA-grade audit semantics, available to you at any time.

Your Apple ID is the only gate

You own the keys, literally. Your iCloud account password (plus two-factor authentication on the trusted devices you've set up with Apple) is the only thing standing between your client data and the world. Voxhora doesn't have a "reset your password" link because Voxhora doesn't have your password — Apple does.

Built for Travis County defense — not generic

Most client-management and billing software is generic: built for corporate or transactional firms, then bent awkwardly to fit a criminal-defense practice. Voxhora is the opposite. It was made specifically for Travis County criminal-defense attorneys — including CAPDS panel attorneys — so the billing categories, the cause-number formats, the court calendar, and the appointment intake all already speak your jurisdiction's language. And every voucher Voxhora generates is built to drop straight into the CAPDS AMP portal — accepted on the first real submission, no reformatting and nothing re-keyed.
